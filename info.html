Story & Approach

Why this work, and how each trip is handled.

Why guiding, not just driving

Many guests arrive here feeling excited but also anxious—about safety, scams, or simply the unpredictability of a big Indian city. Over time, it became clear that what people needed was not just someone to “take them around”, but someone who could quietly hold the space, anticipate needs, and translate the chaos into something beautiful and understandable.

This is the heart of ATITHI DEVO BHAVAH: treating a guest as something sacred. It means showing up on time, answering questions honestly, building plans around your comfort, and remembering that for you, this might be a once-in-a-life experience—not just another day on the road.

How each day is prepared

  • 1 • Listen first Understand your dates, interests, energy level, and any non-negotiables.
  • 2 • Design a calm route Choose a flow with realistic timing, rest stops, and backups.
  • 3 • Safety + clarity Share estimates, live-location option, and what is included/excluded.
  • 4 • Be present On the day, walk with you—not in front, not behind—adapting as needed.
